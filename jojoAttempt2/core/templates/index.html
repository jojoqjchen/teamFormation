How it works

Step 1: Gather basic information about the participants.

This will be used by our algorithm to form the best possible teams, depending on your objective.
You can use any of our templates below, or your own.
Make sure to use numeric values for each of the characteristics you are trying to measure. We recommand using Google Form to gather the information.

Step 2: Upload your data and fill out our 30-second questionnaire.

Once you have uploaded the data obtained during Step 1, you will be prompted some questions that will help the algorithm determine the best team for you.
You will be asked your ideal team size, and whether you want some characteristics to be similar across the teammates, different, or simply ignored.

Step 3: Download and share the results.

You can select to download the final team results in a PDF, CSV, or MS Excel format.

Our templates

Project First (MS Excel) (CSV Version)

In the project first approach, participants enter their ranked preferences for a list of projects. Our "Project First" algorithm will then optimize the teams to maximize the participants’ preferences.

Team First (MS Excel) (CSV Version)

In the team first approach, participants answer some behavioral questions. Our "Team First" algorithm will then optimize the teams to either favor diversity or similarity for each characteristics.

Team Formation Survey (Team First) (Project First)

We recommend using Google Forms to easily generate the results in a CSV or MSExcel format. The questions provided in this survey can be adapted to your own requirements. Note that only numerical answers will be considered for both algorithms.
